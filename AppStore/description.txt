Sozzler puts your favorite cocktails right where you need them: in your pocket. Use Sozzler's minimalist interface to organize and update your personal cocktail recipe library.

Sozzler comes loaded with 42 great recipes, and makes it simple to rank your favorite recipes, modify recipes, add new recipes, and delete recipes you don't like.

Features:
 * Add, update, and delete your recipes
 * Rate your recipes to keep track of your favorites
 * Browse your recipes by name, rating, or ingredient
 * Search your recipes by name or ingredient
 * Import and export your recipes with email
 * Can't decide which cocktail to make? Shake the recipe list and let Sozzler pick one of your favorites for you
 * Customize the ingredients and units of measure used to build your recipes
